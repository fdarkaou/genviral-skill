# Product Context: AnotherWrapper

## What It Is
AnotherWrapper is a Next.js AI starter kit / boilerplate. Pay once ($229-$549), launch unlimited AI-powered SaaS products. It's a codebase, not a service.

## Target Audience
- Indie hackers and solo developers
- AI startup founders who want to ship fast
- Developers tired of setting up auth, payments, and infrastructure from scratch
- "Vibe coders" who use AI coding assistants (Cursor, Claude Code, Codex)

## Key Features
- **Next.js 15** with full TypeScript, SSR, static & dynamic rendering
- **12 AI demo apps** included (text, vision, image generation)
- **AI integrations**: OpenAI, Anthropic, Meta, Google, xAI, DeepSeek
- **Auth**: Multiple OAuth providers
- **Database**: PostgreSQL + Supabase + vector embeddings
- **Payments**: Built-in payment integration (Polar.sh)
- **Styling**: TailwindCSS, daisyUI, shadcn/ui with 40+ themes
- **SEO**: Programmatic SEO, blog support, 1000s of indexed pages
- **Works with AI coding assistants**: Cursor, Claude Code, Copilot, etc.

## Pricing
- **Core**: $229 (OpenAI only)
- **Premium**: $249 (all models, all demo apps, updates) - most popular
- **Enterprise**: $549 (unlimited devs, B2B, commercial)

## Value Proposition
"You don't have to start from scratch." Skip weeks of boilerplate setup. Get auth, payments, analytics, emails, AI integrations, and 12 demo apps out of the box. Focus on your product, not infrastructure.

## Competitive Edge
- 12 working demo apps (not just empty templates)
- Multi-model AI support (not locked to OpenAI)
- One-time payment, unlimited projects
- Built for the "vibe coding" era (works with AI assistants)
